Frequently Asked Questions

How does the installment payment work?

After a small initial deposit, you pay a fixed amount monthly for a set period. No hidden fees. Ownership is transferred to you after the final payment.

What is included in the installation?

Our price includes all components (panels, inverter, batteries), professional installation by our expert team, and post-installation support.

Is there a warranty?

Yes! All our installations come with a warranty. Solar panels typically have a 25-year performance warranty, while inverters and batteries have a 1-5 year warranty depending on the model.
